**Airports:** The local airport is Washington Reagan (also referred to as National Airport/DCA), also within range are IAD – Dulles and BWI – Baltimore. Taxis are easy to locate at all three airports to arrive in the area. The Washington area does have heavy commuter traffic, please build in time prior to your flights to account for these slowdowns. Ubers and Lyft (rideshare services) are readily available in the area but can often have price hikes in response to metro delays.
\\ 
\\  
**Transportation:** The meeting will be held in Bethesda, MD, estimated transit from DCA is approximately 35 minutes (without traffic), 45 minutes (without traffic) from Dulles, and 45 minutes (without traffic) from BWI. Use the WMATA Trip Planner ([[https://www.wmata.com/schedules/trip-planner|https://www.wmata.com/schedules/trip-planner]]) if you are going to use the DC Metro (subway) system to be sure to account for any delays or closings. Ubers and Lyft (rideshare services) are readily available in the area but can often have price hikes in response to metro delays. If you need to take metro, there is a shuttle from the Grosvenor Metro Station on the red line. It leaves from the Kiss & Ride (same side as the parking meters, about 200 feet from the crosswalk from the parking garage). It will say “Capital Gateway”). The shuttle leaves every 20 minutes starting at 6:25 AM and takes about 15 minutes to get to the venue- but please plan to leave extra time as schedules may fluctuate. 
\\ 
\\  
**Neighborhood:** The meeting location is in an office park and as such there are not a lot of food options, though there are a few cafes/small restaurants in nearby buildings {{:documents:resources:lunch_options_updated_to_6710b.pdf|PDF (74 KB)}}. There is a refrigerator in the meeting room if you would like to bring food. The ordering deadline for boxed lunches has passed, but please let us know if you have questions or concerns.
